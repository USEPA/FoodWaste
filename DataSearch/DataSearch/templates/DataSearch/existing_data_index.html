Existing Data Tracking Tool

This tool has been developed to track existing data for 'Plastics Projects' under the new StRAP's assigned to the EDAB branch. Guidance for using data from other sources is available from your Division Quality Assurance Manager (QAM) or from your Center Director of Quality Assurance (DQA). Guidance for Quality Assurance Project Plans (G-5) identifies elements to consider when planning projects that use existing data.

Existing Data and Information Research Projects

An Existing Data and Information research project involves the gathering and/or use of existing environmental data for purposes other than those for which they were originally collected. These Existing Data and Information may be obtained from many sources, including literature, industry surveys, compilations from computerized databases and information systems, and computerized or mathematical models of environmental processes. If primary data will be generated as part of the project, then the information collected can be incorporated into the associated QAPP to address the Existing Data and Information.
