CESER QAPP Builder for Category A or B

ORD OLEM team members will track publicly available existing data for Plastics Projects from articles, abstracts, books, book chapters, computer products, manuals, presentations, proceedings, reports, and scientific data sets. Findings to include attachments will be stored and retrievable in the Strategies for Characterization of Food Waste and Plastics Research database. In addition, users can use the QA R5 QAPP builder to create, store, edit, and export QAPPs.

{% if not user.is_authenticated %} {% endif %}

The database will enable ORD OLEM team members to review and export, as .pdf, .xlxs, and .csv files, existing data obtained from sources, including literature, industry surveys, compilations from computerized databases and information systems, and computerized or mathematical models of environmental processes. If primary data will be generated as part of the project, then information collected will be incorporated into the associated QAPP to address the existing data requirements. For laboratory and/or field environmental measurements project research staff will use the Center for Environmental Solutions & Emergency Response SciNote, ELFN application.
