Notebooks

In the 'Notebooks' section of QA track, you can manage Notebooks.

Select an option from the "Notebook Actions" box to begin.


On a periodic basis, a Notebook custodian or contributor may need to have at least one of their active notebooks reviewed to check they are following standard documentation procedures. If a QAM requests a review, a notification email is sent immediately to initiate scheduling the review. A reminder email is sent on the 15th of each successive month until the review has been performed.
